Simple Tasty Tacos

Everything you'll need and the right steps to take

Ingrediants

  • Ground Beef
  • Rice
  • Shredded cheese of any blend
  • Sour cream
  • Your choice of taco seasoning
  • Ortega taco sauce
  • Crunchy Taco shells

Equipment Needed

  1. Pan
  2. Wooden or plastic spatula
  3. Plates
  4. Forks
  5. Cooking pot

Instructions to Simple Tacos

  1. Place ground beef into heated pan
  2. Once meat starts to brown, place choice of taco seasoning into meat
  3. While meat is continuing to cook, place rice into cooking pot full of water
  4. Once meat has finished cooking, place to the side and begin sturing rice
  5. Once rice has finished cooking, begin building your simple Taco
