Juicy Burger

Easy Ingrediants and Steps to a Juicy Burger

Ingrediants

  • Waygu Beef or your choice of ground beef
  • Your Choice of bun
  • Cheese of any kind
  • Any Condiments of your choice

Equipment Needed For Cooking

  1. Coal
  2. Lighter Fluid
  3. Grill
  4. Spatual

Steps to Success

  1. Place charcoal into a stagering pyrimd shape into the grill
  2. Add lighter fluid to charcoal
  3. light grill and wait 35-45 minutes
  4. Begin molding your waygu beef into paddy form
  5. Season paddies with desired seasoning
  6. Once grill has reached maximum time, place paddies on grill
  7. Grill paddies 5 minuties on each side
  8. Cook burgers until you have a crispy outter skin and a juicy light red inside
  9. Once burgers have finished, place patties onto buns and begin building your master piece
