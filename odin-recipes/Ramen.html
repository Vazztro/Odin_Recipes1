Easy Spicy Ramen

Every Ingrediant,Equipment and Steps to Spicy Ramen

Ingrediants

  • Maruchan Ramen
  • Chives
  • Chile Flakes
  • Sliced Cheese

Equipment Needed

  1. Cooking Pot
  2. Fork
  3. Strainer
  4. Bowl

Instructions to an Amazing Spicy Ramen

  1. Place Maruchan ramen into cooking pot full of water
  2. Place stove to high
  3. While ramen is cooking make sure your other three ingrediants are ready for mixing
  4. Once ramen has gotten soft to the touch, pour ramen into your strainer
  5. Once ramen is in strainer, pour it back into the empty cooking pot and add two tablespoons of water
  6. Add both chives,chili flakes and sliced cheese
  7. Since the ramen is hot, it will both heat up the chives and melt the cheese
  8. Once ingrediants are placed into the ramen, continue to stir, until satisfied
  9. Pour cooked ramen into bowl and ENJOY
